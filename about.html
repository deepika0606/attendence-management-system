About Us

The Attendance Management System is a web-based platform designed to simplify attendance tracking for students and employees. Our system provides a **user-friendly interface** for administrators, teachers, and students to efficiently manage attendance records.

Key Features:

  • Secure login system for administrators, teachers, and students.
  • Automated attendance marking system.
  • Real-time attendance tracking and reporting.
  • Data storage and retrieval with **MySQL database**.
  • Export attendance reports in **CSV and PDF** formats.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide an **efficient and reliable** attendance management solution that enhances productivity, reduces manual work, and ensures **accurate record-keeping**.
